Our Story

Born from a mom's frustration. Built for every family.

In 2019, pediatric nutritionist and mom of three Maya Chen stood in a pharmacy aisle, reading the ingredient list of yet another popular kids' vitamin. Artificial dyes. High-fructose corn syrup. Synthetic fillers. And her daughter still refused to take it.

She thought: there has to be a better way. Two years later, Sprout was born.

The Origin Story

From pharmacy aisle to pediatrician-approved formula

1

The problem no one was solving

After 12 years as a pediatric nutritionist, Maya had seen it all — iron-deficient toddlers, picky eaters with hidden B12 gaps, kids who refused every supplement their parents tried. The market was full of brightly colored gummies loaded with sugar and artificial everything.

Parents were paying premium prices for products that were barely better than candy. And the kids who needed real nutrition the most were the ones most likely to refuse them.

2

Two years in the lab

Maya spent two years working with a team of pediatric nutritionists, food scientists, and child development specialists to create something genuinely different. The breakthrough came from an unlikely place: food flavor research. Kids weren't refusing vitamins because of the vitamins — they were refusing the artificial aftertaste.

By using whole-food flavor compounds and natural sweeteners instead of synthetic alternatives, Maya's team created the first chewable vitamin that kids actually asked for by name.

3

The beta test that changed everything

In 2020, Maya ran a 90-day beta test with 200 families across 8 cities. The results were staggering: 94% of kids took their vitamin without being asked after the first week. Within 60 days, parents reported measurable improvements in energy, sleep quality, and school focus in 78% of children.

One parent wrote: "My daughter called it her 'magic berry.' I've never seen her excited about anything health-related." That note became the founding mission of Sprout.

4

50,000 families and counting

Sprout launched officially in January 2021. Within 6 months, it had become the fastest-growing children's supplement brand in North America. Today, over 50,000 families subscribe monthly, and Sprout has expanded to a full range of pediatric formulas — each one carrying the same founding promise: clean, effective nutrition that kids actually love.
